Job Description:

Start Date:immediately or by mutual agreement

Bern

20% Remote Work

As a strong leader, you, together with your team, ensure ICT procurement, licensing, and contract management. You supply the cantonal administration with all necessary ICT services and goods needed for the digital transformation of their business processes. You have a lot of flexibility in shaping internal processes and structures, and you can contribute your expertise and experience.

Your responsibilities

  • Ensure, harmonize, and further develop ICT procurement in the KAIO and in the cantonal administration
  • Lead the five-person procurement team both personnel and technically
  • Ensure and manage cantonal ICT contract and license management
  • Lead and/or participate in ICT procurement projects

Your profile

  • Higher business education
  • Experience and education in public procurement
  • Several years of leadership experience
  • Strong communication skills and assertiveness

We offer you

  • Extensive …

professional freedom and development opportunities

  • Modern work equipment and a workplace within walking distance of Bern’s main train station
  • Advanced working conditions, good social benefits, and interesting training opportunities

Contact

We look forward to receiving your online application under the link “Apply Now”. For information, please contact Mrs. Diana Nyffenegger, Head of Resources + Procurement, Telephone

Company Details

The Office for Informatics and Organization (KAIO) is the competence center for implementing digitalization in the cantonal administration. With standardized services in the areas of organization, security, applications, workplaces, and infrastructure, we offer our customers the opportunity to digitally transform their business and focus entirely on their core business. We also ensure central ICT basic services throughout the canton
