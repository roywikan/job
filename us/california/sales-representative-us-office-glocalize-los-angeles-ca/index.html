Responsibilities:

We are seeking a highly motivated individual to join our team in Singapore as a Sales Representative. The successful candidate will possess excellent communication and organizational skills, be a team player, and a self-starter. The ideal candidate must have a proven sales record and relevant experience in the localization media industry. Relationship management and business development skills are essential, and the ability to build strong relationships with clients is a must. The candidate must be results-driven, target-oriented, and able to work independently and as part of a team.

Responsibilities include presenting, promoting, and selling our products and services to existing and potential clients, analyzing customer requirements, and developing a comprehensive localization solution for each client. The Sales Representative will be expected to generate leads through cold calling and emailing and maintain positive client relationships. The candidate must meet sales targets and coordinate sales efforts with other team members and departments. Additionally, the candidate will be responsible for analyzing market potential, tracking sales, and reporting on customer needs and interests, as well as competitive activities and potential for new products and services.

The ideal candidate will have excellent problem-solving capabilities, attention to detail, and the ability to work under pressure and meet deadlines. Strong selling, negotiation, and communication skills are essential, as well as proficiency in English (both verbal and written). The ability to create and deliver sales decks tailored to clients’ needs and knowledge of additional languages are an advantage. The Sales Representative must continuously improve through feedback from clients and peers and stay up-to-date with best practices and trends in the localization industry.

 

 

Job Description:

GloZ, a globalization company that empowers stories, is looking for a highly motivated Sales Representative to join their team in their US office. The successful candidate must be able to work independently, possess strong communication and organizational skills, and be a great team player.

As a Sales Representative, the candidate will be responsible for promoting and selling GloZ’s products and services to existing and prospective clients. The job requires the ability to perform cost-benefit analysis, establish and maintain positive business relationships, generate leads, and work with the team to resolve customer complaints and problems.

To be considered for the job, the candidate must have proven sales experience in the localization media industry, excellent communication and negotiation skills, and the ability to work independently and as part of a team. Other requirements include prioritizing, time management, and organizational skills, and the ability to meet deadlines under pressure.

GloZ encourages continuous improvement, so the Sales Representative must be open to feedback and possess problem-solving capabilities. They must also be detail-oriented, self-starter, and proactive, with excellent verbal and written English communication skills. Knowledge of an additional language is an advantage.

The Sales Representative must be highly motivated, target-driven, and have a proven track record in sales. They should also be able to create and deliver sales presentations tailored to clients’ needs while keeping up-to-date with best practices and trends in the localization industries.
